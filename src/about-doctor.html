About DOCTOR
Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD)
DR. VLADIMIR PASTOUK
Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD)
DR. VLADIMIR PASTOUK

Dr. Pastouk has been working in dentistry for two decades now, and he has no intention of stopping anytime soon. He takes pride in being a health problem solver for people in need! His exceptional reputation and unsurpassed, world-class care are responsible for the success of Cleveland Center of Integrative Dentistry, and he strives to deliver perfection in every smile.

Dr. Pastouk first graduated from the Kyiv Medical University School of Dentistry in Ukraine in 1991. After relocating to the US, he worked at the University Hospitals of Cleveland and participated in thousands of medical and dental surgeries. He was also a part of the surgical team at the highest-risk operating room of the Labor and Delivery Department at one time, successfully delivering over 500 kids through C-sections.

Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD)
  • Board Certified Naturopathic Physician (N.D.)
  • Board certified in Integrative Biologic Dental Medicine (I.B.D.M.)
  • Accredited Member of International Academy of Oral Medicine and Toxicology
  • Diplomate of International Congress of Oral Implantologists
  • Associate Fellow of American Academy of Implant Dentistry
  • Fellow of Misch International Implant Institute
  • Sustained Member of American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry
  • VP, Director of Clinical Education at Pacific Training Institute of Facial Aesthetics

After acquiring a tremendous amount of experience in surgery, he chose to dedicate himself to dentistry (focusing on implants and cosmetics in particular) and attended The Advanced Standing Program at the College of Dentistry at Nova Southeastern University in Florida. Later, he also completed a year-long program in modern dental implantology and advanced bone grafting in Atlanta.

Last 15 years Dr. Pastouk dedicated himself to Biological Dentistry - Providing Comprehensive Expertise in Diagnosing and treating his patients on the bases of Biological dentistry in conjunction with general health symptoms and conditions. Such an experience and research Dr. Pastouk reflected in his unique subject development - Glossоdontics™.

He also gives back to others and helps rejuvenate smiles whenever possible, including a trip to treat underprivileged kids in the Dominican Republic in 2001 and yearly work with the Give Back a Smile program that helps improve dental health for victims of domestic abuse. Dr. Pastouk has taken an active role in the biological surgical treatment of worldwide patients in the dental establishment in Costa Rica since 2019.

Dr. Pastouk is originally from Ternopil in Ukraine and is proud to call Cleveland his hometown today. He is happily married. When he isn’t busy at the dental office, his personal interests include reading, traveling, and sports.
